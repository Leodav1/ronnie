Ronnie Radke

Ronnie Radke performing live.

Ronald Joseph Radke (born December 15, 1983) is a renowned musician and songwriter known for his influential contributions to the post-hardcore and metalcore genres. As the frontman of Falling In Reverse, he has left a lasting impact on the music scene.

  • 2004: Ronnie Radke formed the band "Escape the Fate" which also included Max Green, Robert Ortiz and Omar Espinosa. Their career was launched after they won a radio contest which was judged by My Chemical Romance.
  • 2006-2010: Because of some personal issues with his band, Radke decided to form a new band in late 2006. With the help of his friend Nason Schoeffler, he formed a band named "Behind the Walls". Later it was renamed to "Falling in Reverse". In 2008, Radke was arrested and he spent some time in prison. He was released in December 2010.
  • 2011: He resumed his work with Falling in Reverse and the band's first album "The Drug in Me Is You" was released on 26 July 2011 through Epitaph Records. The album was a huge success, peaking at Number 19 on the US Billboard 200. It also appeared on the top albums charts in several other countries, such as Australia, Canada and the UK.
  • 2012-2013: Radke made an announcement that he was working on his second studio album. After the launch of his YouTube channel in 2013, he released a solo song "The Fairweather Fans". Soon after the release of his second song "What Up Earth?, his band's second album "Fashionably Late" was released in June 2013.
  • 2015-2017: The band's third and fourth albums "Just Like You" and "Coming Home" were released in February 2015 and April 2017 respectively. Both the works were moderately successful.
  • 2020-present: Falling in Reverse's first two albums reached the top 20 in the United States, and all four have charted in the top 40. The band's song "Popular Monster" became a No. 1 hit on Billboard's Mainstream Rock chart in 2020, their first chart-topper.he has stated that he is in the process of making a rap mixtape, which according to Radke will include an artist on every song. He has also stated that the mixtape may be released for around Christmas.
